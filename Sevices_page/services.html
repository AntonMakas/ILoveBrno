Transport

Public Transport

Brno’s public transport, managed by DPMB, is an efficient system of trams, buses, and trolleybuses connecting the city. Tickets can be purchased at stops, onboard, or via mobile apps and must be validated when boarding. Trams are the main mode of transport, while buses and trolleybuses cover suburban areas. Night services operate from midnight to 5 a.m., and the system is accessible, with vehicles equipped for reduced mobility and clear stop announcements. Brno continues to modernize its eco-friendly fleet and expand routes for better connectivity.
